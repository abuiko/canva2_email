Your Challenge: Design a T-shirt

We all have T-shirts that show how much we love certain bands, TV shows, sports teams, or hobbies. For this week’s #CanvaDesignChallenge, use your imagination to create a unique T-shirt that’s never been seen before. Go wild with colors, images, text, graphics, and patterns, then share your design with us. Our favorites will win a Canva prize pack. You can even opt to get your design printed on a T-shirt and delivered to your doorstep.
